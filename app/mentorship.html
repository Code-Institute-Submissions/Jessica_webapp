Jessica Traynor

Mentorship

Aside from my own writing, my professional career to date has been concerned with writer development. I very much enjoy working with aspiring, emerging and established writers both in a group setting or one-to-one. I’ve given workshops to groups at various events around Ireland, and am always keen to take on new engagements.

Have a look at the options below and get in touch if you’d like to explore any of them in further detail, or to invite me to give a workshop at your festival or event.

Mentorship

I offer one-to-one mentorship on poetry and plays through the Irish Writers Centre. You can find details here.

I also offer a play-reading service through Big Smoke Writing Factory.

Creative Writing Courses

Keep an eye on my homepage for updated on creative writing courses with Big Smoke Writing Factory and the Irish Writers Centre.

Dramaturgy

To enquire about dramaturgical work on a theatre project, please contact me directly.
